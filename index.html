What is the VOID??

The Void is an anonymous place to go when there is no other place to go. There is no censorship or limits here, just the freedom to speak your find and look into the posts to see how else the world is doing. Feel Free to give it a try, or just take a moment to relax and read what others are saying around you.

Who is in the Void?

The Void can be anyone. It might be a teenager whom feels like the world doesn’t understand them, or a adult whom is looking for a place to kill time while at work. The void doesn’t care who is behind the screen or where you come from, it is only here to listen.
